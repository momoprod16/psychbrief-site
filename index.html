Official service information

This is the official website for the Psych Brief app. Psych Brief uses TikTok developer tools only to connect an authorized creator account (Login Kit, user.info.basic) and to upload creator-owned video drafts to the creator’s TikTok inbox (Content Posting API, video.upload). The creator reviews and posts every draft inside TikTok. Psych Brief does not scrape TikTok and does not automate TikTok Studio.
